About Me


My name is Tony and I'm from County Clare in Ireland. I studied a bachelor of science in multimedia and computer games development at the University of Limerick and have worked in support and customer success roles in the tech industry for over 5 years. Having completed a Masters of science in Interaction and Experience Design at the University of Limerick and receiving first class honours, I’ve now moved towards working in design. Working directly with customers in relation to products has given me great experience understanding and empathising with users as well as well as an abundance of experience directly engaging with engineering and product teams on a daily basis.

I've always had a keen appreciation for art and design and I'm fascinated by Human Computer interaction. At the end of 2020 I decided to make the move towards working in design when I applied for a UX Masters at UL. I coded this website to showcase my progress and interest in design as well as to sharpen my existing programming, web design and interaction design skills. I'm also passionate about Golf, Music and I love to travel.
