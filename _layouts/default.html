About Me


My name is Tony and I'm from County Clare in Ireland. I studied a bachelor of science in multimedia and computer games design at the University of Limerick and have worked in support and customer success roles in the tech industry for over 5 years. Having completed a Masters of science in Interaction and Experience Design at the University of Limerick and receiving first class honours, I now plan to work in design.

I'm passionate about Golf, Music and I love to travel. I've always had a keen appreciation for art and design and I'm fasicnated by Human Computer interaction. At the end of 2020 I decided to make the move towards working in design when I applied for a UX Masters at UL. I coded this website to showcase my progress and interest in design as well as to sharpen my existing programming, web design and interaction design skills.
